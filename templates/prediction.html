🧠 How These ML Models Work
Logistic Regression (Sprint Prediction)

Learns a mathematical boundary between "success" and "failure" sprint patterns. Outputs a probability score between 0–1. Easy to interpret and explain. Trained on 50 synthetic sprint records with features: velocity, tasks, bugs, capacity.

Random Forest (Task Priority)

Builds 100 decision trees, each trained on random subsets of data. Final prediction is the majority vote. Handles non-linear patterns well. Features: complexity, deadline, business value, dependencies.

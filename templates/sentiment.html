🧠 TextBlob Sentiment Analysis — How It Works
Step 1: Tokenization

TextBlob breaks the sentence into individual words (tokens). Each word is assigned a sentiment score from a built-in lexicon database.

Step 2: Polarity Score

Words like "excellent", "great" get positive scores (+1). Words like "terrible", "failed" get negative scores (-1). Average is computed.

Step 3: Classification

Polarity > 0.1 → Positive
Polarity < -0.1 → Negative
Otherwise → Neutral
